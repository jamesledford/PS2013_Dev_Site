Planet Studio was tasked with the design and development of an interactive sales app for ECOLAB’s Temperature Management line of equipment. Originally limited to accessing PDF documents to share with both existing and potential customers, ECOLAB’s Temperature Management Sales Team wanted a resource that would be more dynamic and was able to capture new, compelling footage and imagery of the equipment, inclusive of showcasing the various machines in a hospital setting. With this as the end goal, it was important that we create a structure that would be able to deliver the content in a way that would help the salespeople more efficiently sell the features and benefits of the various products within the Temperature Management division.





Custom App Experience

In collaborating with ECOLAB and reviewing existing sales and marketing pieces, Planet Studio shaped the design of an easy to navigate, customized iPad app, which features both an overview of the full Temperature Management line and individual product tabs that house product-specific videos, dynamic links, and other reference material that will help sell in the products and create an attractive buy for customers.


Video Production

In order to supplement the app, Planet Studio filmed and edited a five minute Temperature Management Overview video and four product-specific videos for ECOLAB’s IntraTemp Fluid Warming Systems, ORS Fluid Warming Systems, Scope Pillow, and Hush Slush. These videos, all filmed inside a real Operating Room, live within the app, but they can also be used as standalone pieces for conferences, client-facing meetings, etc. Finally, Planet Studio captured high resolution photographs of all products for print and promotional use, so that ECOLAB’s Temperature Management team will have the additional resources available to showcase their products as needed.
